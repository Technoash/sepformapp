Welcome to perForm

Form Management System


The core mission of perForm is to allow small, growing businesses and large businesses alike the ability to manage their forms and paperwork in an intuitive, lightweight, online platform.

Managers can set up new forms for their employees to fill out. Once completed Managers will be notified so they can review the form and provide feedback

Need to send a quick message to a colleague? Maybe there you want to attach some comments to a submission? perForm allows you to quickly make remarks on submissions to save you time.

As your business grows so will your perForm. Easily add new Employees and new Managers to grow your online forms system.
